ABOUT SRI LANKA

Sri Lanka is the tear shaped island that seems to hang off from the subcontinent of India. Often referred to as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean, Sri Lanka is a paradise waiting to be discovered. With a turbulent history which goes back to the beginning of time. Sri Lanka is most famous for its incredible nature, beautiful tropical beaches, breathtaking mountains, charming people, inspiring heritage sites and wildlife parks. With so much of diversity in such a small island, Sri Lanka is bound to surprise and delight all types of travelers.

There are very few places in the world where you can indulge in such a variety of experiences: soaking the sun on a golden beach, trekking up a cool hillside terrain, venturing into dense rainforests, witnessing an elephant gathering, wildlife safaris, envisaging the magnificently rich history of the islandâ€¦ the list goes on. The culture of Sri Lanka is as vibrant as its sights. The people are friendly and famous for their inimitable Sri Lankan Hospitality.
